Steps for syncing an update to the Screensteps manual to VLP

Start with the knowledge that there is NO true sync. If you edit a manual on VLP it does not and will not update in Screensteps, and visa versa. Update the manual in Screensteps and you have to manually update it in VLP

The VLP manual editing is a promotion process.

As seen in the pic above the three states from bottom to top are:

  • Imported
  • Draft
  • Published

Each steps has different actions that can be taken

To get the updates live for the students the imported state needs to be promoted to Draft, then Draft to Published.

Once you Publish the Draft (promote draft to published) the student will get a notice that it has changed.

The part of updating the manual that is NOT very intuitive is that you begin by ADDING A MANUAL, then importing from Screensteps, even though exists
