The DNS/NTP Servers for attendee facing use (Horizon Desktops, vPod external resolution) are as follows:

  • 172.25.0.10
  • 172.25.0.11

vPod Time should be set to 192.168.100.1 (ntp.corp.local). This is the vPodRouter.

The vPodRouter gets its time from the ESXi Host it is running on and acts as an NTP Server itself for the vPod. All ESXi Hosts in the lab environment get their time from the Lab AD Servers, and the AD Servers get them from pool.ntp.org .
